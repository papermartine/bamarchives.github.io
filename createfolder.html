Setting up a Folder

  1. Right Click on folder where you would like to create a sub-folder.
  2. Select "New Folder"
  3. Title the newly created folder while it is highlighted, or double click to make the text editable.

When in doubt, contact the archives! Feel free to email any questions or concerns to archives@bam.org.
